Who we are

The UK Government Web Archive team is part of The National Archives. We are a dedicated group of archivists, developers, and digital preservation specialists committed to preserving the UK's online government heritage.

Our team works collaboratively with government departments across the UK to ensure comprehensive coverage of government web presence.

What we do

Our responsibilities include:

  • Identifying and selecting government websites for archiving
  • Operating web crawling infrastructure
  • Ensuring long-term preservation of archived content
  • Providing public access to the archive
